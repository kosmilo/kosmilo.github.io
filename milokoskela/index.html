Upside Down

Upside Down is a simple platformer made in Unity. In the game the player has to solve puzzles and touch the green platform to get to the next level. It also has a cubes that player can push around and two other kinds of platforms; a blue one that makes the gravity opposite, and a pink one which makes the corresponding platform or wall disapear.
